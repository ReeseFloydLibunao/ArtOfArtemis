The Art of Artemis

Residency Details on how she was born and given everything she rules over. Born out of an affair with Zeus, and her momma being chased by a giant snake, her story of birth surely is fascinating and complex. She resides over many things. From hunting deer and wildlife in the forest, to protecting girls until they are married, to even being responsible of making sure people are born correctly. This girl resides it all, even the moon belongs to her (in some obscure versions of mythology). More Information!

Orion A quite famous son of Poseidon, being a very popular constellation and all. He was a very influential figure in Artemis’ life. A close friend with Artemis, our dear Orion was. Despite her friend group primarily consisting of mostly nymphs and forest creatures, she befriended this kind and gentle son of Poseidon. Until tragedy, in the form of someone’s deception, struck the poor giant. In the form of a familiar golden arrow fired from a silver bow….

Worship How and why she was worshipped, from sacrificing as always to making beautiful statues and paintings of her. Including her representation in media from videogames (mostly Hades (great game)) to movies (I’ll watch Artemis Fowl to see if she’s represented well there (probably not, just guessing from the reviews). She was mainly worshipped trough sacrifices and prayers of course, like any other god. And worshipping a deity responsible for making sure you are fruitful in the hunt of food you need to, y’know, stay alive, is pretty important. Giving birth is also pretty important, we should probably praise the god who oversees that process.

Minor Roles A few of the minor roles she had in mythology as a whole. Helping against a war against giants, some kids stacked mountains on top of each other to reach Olympus; Hercules was trying to redeem himself, from turning a girl named Callisto, who broke a vow of chastity, into a bear or something. She has appeared a handful of times in other stories, though she herself is very distant from Olympus, preferring to spend more time in the woods with her girlfriends. She still has many minor roles in others’ stories.
